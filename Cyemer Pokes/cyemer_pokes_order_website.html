Welcome to Cyemer Pokes

Cyemer Pokes is a student-run Pokémon card resale group serving students of Amjad School – Lebanon. We sell real Pokémon cards, bundles, and accept fair trades.

All orders are placed online and confirmed through WhatsApp to keep things simple and safe.
